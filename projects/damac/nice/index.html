About the project

Nice - luxury resort in Provence style

Inspired by the Maritime Alps on the Cote d’Azur of France, the new Nice complex has become the pearl of the emirate. It’s no secret that Impressionist painters have always been fascinated by this small coastal town. The developer DAMAC also found inspiration in this French town and embodied it in the residential complex of the same name.

Symbiosis of picturesque views and excellent architecture

Nice is a real work of art for those who are used to enjoying sunny skies, blue waters and watching sunsets. Nice is the starting point for spectacular journeys along the picturesque Cote d’Azur, now people in the UAE are closer to this paradise than ever before. The new complex is located in a good location, has excellent transport interchange and boasts walking distance to shops, beauty salons and other services.
